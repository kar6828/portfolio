For this Corpus Analysis assignment, I compared the fictional story of Dracula to a series of historic texts (COHA-historic-to-pres). Being a dated story itself, I found many similar words between Dracula and the other texts. The most common among these "dated" was the word "shall". I believe this similarity shows that dialogue plays a large part in all of these texts.

Image of my Analysis in Voyant
4-Grams from Dracula
Another similarity I noticed during my analysis was the mysterious environment created in the majority of these texts. Two very common 4-Grams in Dracula are "i could see that" and "i do not know". This implies that a large part of the story, Dracula, is spent discerning what is known and what is not. It seems like a lot of the dated texts I analized are mysterious!
